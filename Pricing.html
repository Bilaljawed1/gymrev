Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Is there a joining fee? +
No joining fee, ever. We believe in transparent pricing — what you see is what you pay. Your first session is always free so you can experience Gym Evolution before committing to any plan.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. All monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Yearly plans can be paused or cancelled with a 30-day notice. No contracts, no drama.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +
You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. Our team will handle everything for you.
Is the free trial really free? +
100% free — no credit card required. Come in, meet our coaches, use the facilities, and try a class. We're confident you'll love it. If not, no hard feelings.
Do you offer student or corporate discounts? +
Yes! We offer 15% off for students with valid ID and corporate rates for teams of 5 or more. Contact our team directly to set up a corporate account for your company.
What if I want more PT sessions than my plan includes? +
Additional personal training sessions can be purchased à la carte at $55/session, or you can upgrade to the Champion plan for unlimited PT access with a dedicated coach.
