About US

We didn't build a gym. We built a revolution. Born from a relentless belief that everyone deserves access to elite training, Gym Evolution has grown from a single room to the city's premier fitness destination — and we're just getting started.

How It All Began

Born From Passion.
Built For Champions.

In 2015, founder and former professional athlete Derek Shaw walked away from a corporate fitness chain with one conviction: people deserve better. He opened the original Gym Evolution in a 3,000 sq ft warehouse with second-hand equipment and a first-class mindset.

Word spread fast. Not because of marketing — but because results spoke louder than any ad campaign. Members were transforming. Coaches were coaching. And a community was forming that had never existed in a gym before.

Today, Gym Evolution spans 28,000 sq ft of state-of-the-art training space, serves over 5,000 active members, and employs 30+ elite coaches. The spirit, however, remains exactly the same — push further, evolve daily, never settle.

Our Mission

To unlock the extraordinary in every person who walks through our doors.

We believe fitness isn't a privilege — it's a right. Our mission is to create an environment where every person, regardless of fitness level, background, or experience, is empowered to push past their perceived limits and discover what they're truly made of.


Every program we design, every coach we hire, and every piece of equipment we install is chosen with one question in mind: does this help our members evolve? If the answer is yes, we do it. If not, we don't.

Our Journey

A Decade of Evolution

2015 · The Beginning
The Warehouse Gym Opens

Derek Shaw signs the lease on a 3,000 sq ft warehouse in the industrial district. With 50 charter members and a vision, Gym Evolution opens its doors for the first time.

2017 · Growth
First Major Expansion

Membership triples in two years. Gym Evolution expands to 10,000 sq ft, adds a dedicated functional training zone, and hires its first team of 8 full-time coaches.

2019 · Recognition
Named #1 Gym in the City

Two consecutive years of recognition as the city's top-rated training facility. The Evolution method attracts national attention and the waiting list hits 400 people.

2021 · Innovation
Evolution App & Digital Launch

We launch the Gym Evolution app, bringing personalized programming, nutrition tracking, and live coaching to members' phones — training without boundaries.

2025 · The Now
28,000 Sq Ft. 5,000 Members. Still Hungry.

Our current flagship facility stands as the premier training destination in the region. We've helped thousands transform their bodies — and our ambition has never been greater.
